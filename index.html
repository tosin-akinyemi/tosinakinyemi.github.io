Biography

About Me

I am Esther Akinyemi — a Healthcare Compliance Specialist and Project Coordinator based in Toronto, Ontario. With over five years of experience across telecom, hospitality, and technology sectors, I specialize in driving customer success, managing complex compliance frameworks, and coordinating cross-functional teams to deliver measurable results.

I am currently pivoting into healthcare compliance — leveraging deep expertise in HIPAA, PIPEDA, and GCP cloud security to help healthcare organizations build robust, ethical, and secure compliance frameworks that enable rather than restrict innovation.

My academic background includes a Master's in Informatics with a mixed concentration in Cloud Computing and Information Security from Northeastern University, a Diploma in Digital Business Management from Toronto School of Management, and a Bachelor's in Sociology and Anthropology.
